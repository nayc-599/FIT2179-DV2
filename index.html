3. Where in Australia Are They Leaving From?

Emigrant departures are not evenly spread across Australia — they flow overwhelmingly from the states where migrant communities are most concentrated. New South Wales, Victoria and Queensland account for the vast majority of all departures, consistent with their populations and international connectivity. Every state shows the same sharp COVID dip in 2020–21, followed by a gradual recovery that is still incomplete.

New South Wales accounts for the largest share of national departures — home to Sydney, Australia's most globally connected city and the first stop for many newly arrived migrants.
